Description

Epigenomics is an emerging frontier of science. This short course will cover current topics in epigenomics, including modern epigenomics technologies and their data analysis in the context of integration of big bio-data.

Syllabus

  1. Genes, genome, and modern genomics
    1. Gene regulation
    2. Current bioinformatics and genomics
    3. High-throughput sequencing
  2. Epigenetic mechanisms
    1. DNA methylation
    2. Histone code
    3. RNA components of the epigenome
    4. Interplay between genome and epigenome
  3. Epigenome and life
    1. Development
    2. Health and diseases
    3. Epigenetics in cancer
    4. Environmental epigenomics
  4. Technology in modern epigenomics
    1. Epigenomic approaches
    2. Sequencing based technology
    3. Analysis of epigenomic data
  5. From genome to epigenome to genome 4D
    1. Chromatin biology
    2. Genome and epigenome architecture
    3. Epigenetic annotation of the genome
    4. Higher order of the genome
  6. Modern epigenomic resources – epigenome and big bio-data
    1. ENCODE
    2. Roadmap epigenomics
    3. 4D nucleome network
    4. The international epigenomics community
